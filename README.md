# 🎂 Interactive Birthday Cake Blow 🎉

A fun interactive birthday cake where you blow out candles using your microphone!

💝 **Project is dedicated to my partner**

## ✨ Features

- 🕯️ **24 Interactive Candles** - Randomly positioned on the cake
- 🎤 **Microphone Detection** - Blow into your device to extinguish candles
- 🎊 **Confetti Animation** - Colorful celebration when all candles are out
- 🎵 **Happy Birthday Music** - Auto-plays with toggleable on/off button
- 🔄 **Restart Button** - Play again anytime

## 🚀 How to Use

1. [Click this](https://jhieminn.github.io/birthday-cake-blow/) and open in your browser
2. Allow microphone access
3. Blow into your microphone to extinguish the candles
4. Enjoy the celebration with confetti and music!
5. Click restart to play again

## 💻 Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3 (with animations)
- JavaScript (Web Audio API)
- Confetti.js
